Fake news is everywhere around us, it's on our televisions, it's on our phones, and it floods the media on a daily basis. Fake news can range from absolute deceit towards the general public by news companies to small changes or ways news is expressed to the public to cause fear or even reduce fear. Young and old people alike must be made aware of the presence of fake news and understand how to identify it and single it out from real news, through research and understanding of world events.
